Our Focus Areas

01. Athletics & Leadership

Youth Sportsmanship

We believe that the field of play is the greatest classroom for life. Through our sports programs, we mentor young athletes not just to win, but to lead with integrity, teamwork, and discipline. By providing equipment and professional coaching, we keep youth engaged and healthy.

Active Projects:
  • Annual EAF Peace Tournament
  • Community Sports Equipment Drive
02. Compassionate Care

Supporting the Elderly

Our elders are the pillars of our community, yet they are often the most vulnerable. EAF provides direct support to the elderly through nutritional programs, home visits, and health check-ups. We ensure that no senior citizen in our reach feels forgotten or abandoned.

Active Projects:
  • Building Houses for the elderly
  • Senior Health & Wellness Camps
